About YouthLight Foundation

YouthLight Foundation was established in 2008 with a simple yet powerful vision: to ensure every child, regardless of their background, has access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Over the past 16 years, we have grown from a small community initiative to a recognized organization impacting thousands of lives across multiple states. Our holistic approach combines education, nutrition, healthcare, and skill development to create lasting change.
